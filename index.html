About Montana Student Accommodation

Welcome to Montana Student Accommodation, your trusted home away from home. We are proudly accredited by NSFAS (National Student Financial Aid Scheme) and specifically cater to University of Venda students.

Our accommodation provides a safe, comfortable, and conducive environment for academic success. We understand the unique needs of university students and have designed our facilities and services to support your educational journey.

At Montana Student Accommodation, we believe that quality housing should be accessible and affordable. That's why we work closely with NSFAS to ensure eligible students can focus on their studies without worrying about accommodation costs.
